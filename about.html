How we generate revenue

LX Obsidian Labs operates on a multi-channel revenue model, delivering value to clients while building sustainable, recurring income streams.

Subscription Development

Monthly retainer-based software development for web, mobile, and cloud. Starter ($2,499/mo), Growth ($6,999/mo), and Enterprise (custom). Recurring revenue with predictable cash flow.

App Store Sales

One-time and subscription purchases of premium software from the LX Obsidian App Store. Prices range from $19.99 to $79.99 per license. Volume discounts and enterprise licensing available.
